POS Profit in sale detail

Currently, profit and cost subtotal not there in the pos summary report. So here we build a module that provides you profit details, cost subtotal, discount amount, subtotal, cost price in the sales details report.

Features

You can easily calculate sales profit.

Easily get a discounted amount in the pos sales details report.

Easily get cost price in pos sales details report.

Easily get cost subtotal amount in pos sales details report.

Easily get the subtotal amount in the pos sales details report.

No, any special configuration required, install it and use it.
